## Slovenia/EU Inflation (SURS, Eurostat)
- 2020: ~1.2% (SI), 0.7% (EU avg)
- 2021: ~2.0% (SI), 2.9% (EU)
- 2022: ~8.8% (SI), 9.2% (EU) — historical high
- 2023: ~7.4% (SI)
- 2024: ~2.4% (SI)
- 2025: ~2.7% (SI), 2.4% avg
- Cumulative 2020-2025: roughly 25% total inflation in Slovenia
- Sources: SURS stat.si, Eurostat HICP, ECB data portal

## Silver Market Fundamentals
- 5th consecutive year of structural deficit (2021-2025)
- Cumulative deficit: ~820 million ounces
- 2025 deficit: ~95-117 Moz
- Industrial demand at record levels (solar, electronics)
- Source: Silver Institute
