## 7. Negative Interest Rate Era (2014-2022) Impact

### ECB Policy
- First implemented: June 2014
- Lowest rate: -0.50% (2019-2022)
- Ended: September 2022

### Effects (Academic Research)
1. **Bank profitability:** Direct cost €15 billion/year to European banks
2. **Lending impact:** Mixed - initially boosted, then constrained
3. **Sovereign debt:** Facilitated debt buildup (governments paid to borrow)
4. **Asset prices:** Pushed investors toward riskier assets
