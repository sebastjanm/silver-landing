# Research: Srebro vs Bitcoin vs Nepremičnine

## Key Data Points (verified)

### Silver
- Current price: ~€65/oz (~€2.10/g) (Feb 2026, from DAILY-INTEL)
- 10-year performance: silver went from ~$15/oz (2016) to ~$32/oz (2026) = roughly doubled
- No capital gains tax in Slovenia after 2 years ownership
- 22% DDV on physical silver (avoidable through bonded warehouse like Elementum)
- Entry: can start from 1 oz (~€65)
- Volatility: moderate, 3.6x less volatile than bitcoin (BlackRock/iShares data)

### Bitcoin
- Current price: ~€59,200 (Yahoo Finance, Feb 15 2026)
- ATH: €107,662 — currently down ~45% from ATH
- 2022 crash: dropped ~75% from ATH (~$69k to ~$16k)
- Volatility: 3.6x gold, 5.1x global equities (BlackRock)
- Entry: fractional buying possible (from €10)
- Tax in Slovenia: 10% flat tax on crypto gains (after FURS clarification)
- No physical asset — digital only

### Nepremičnine (Real Estate)
- SURS Q3 2025: +2.7% year-on-year, but -1.1% quarter-on-quarter
- SURS Q2 2025: new apartments +11.5% 
- 10-year trend: prices more than doubled (Informer.si, Jan 2026)
- 2025: new residential lost ~10% value (Informer.si)
- Entry: typically €100,000+ (or more in Ljubljana)
- Very illiquid — months to sell
- Ongoing costs: maintenance, taxes, insurance
- Capital gains tax: 25% (decreasing over time, 0% after 15 years)
