## Key Mistakes to Cover:

1. **Buying numismatic instead of bullion** 
   - Premiums on collector coins can be 50-200%+ over spot
   - Bullion coins/bars trade closer to spot

2. **Ignoring storage costs and logistics**
   - Home storage risks (theft, fire)
   - Bank vaults have fees
   - Allocated vs unallocated storage

3. **Not understanding premiums**
   - Dealer spread (buy/sell difference)
   - Premium over spot varies by product
   - Small coins have higher premiums per oz

4. **Buying paper silver instead of physical**
   - ETFs have counterparty risk
   - Futures contracts expire
   - "Unallocated" accounts may not be fully backed

5. **Emotional buying (timing mistakes)**
   - Buying at price peaks out of FOMO
   - Panic selling at lows
   - Dollar-cost averaging as solution
